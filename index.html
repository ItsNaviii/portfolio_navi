Hi, my name is Navi and I’m a 3D artist from Spain. I’ve been creating models since 2022, and during that time, I’ve had the opportunity to work on several amazing projects. Below, I’ll showcase some of my most recent work, as well as the YouTubers and content creators I’ve collaborated with. I have experience in 3D modeling and texturing, as well as in creating texture packs for Java. I also know how to integrate 3D models into TexturePacks, add Unicodes, and work with various resources related to this type of development. (Optifine / ETF, EMF)
